Teddy Triebwasser

Hello, I'm Teddy and I'm a product designer at heart. I like the process of empathizing with users and discovering pain-points through UX research. I also like to incorporate creative interaction design patterns to create product solutions. I've spent a lot of time pondering about how to improve user experience, whether that is an e-commerce application, a travel backpack, or even how to orchestrate the best pizza night at home!
